What is recycling?

recycling is the process of accumulating and redeveloping the used garbage and turning them into something usefull. we are opting for this, because it is one of the best methods to reduce waste.

  • reduce the quantity of landfill.
  • Conserves natural resources such as timber, water and minerals
  • Increases economic security by tapping a domestic source of materials
  • Prevents pollution by reducing the need to collect new raw materials
  • reduce air pollution and water pollution
  • reduces greenhouse gas emissions
  • more sace for other uses
